Kirk Cousins, Minnesota Viking, Quarterback

Captain Kirk has been in the NFL since 2012, taking over at helm for the Vikings in 2018. Cousins has been a solid to later quality QB in Fantasy Football.

However, two important changes have upped his potential. The first being Coach Kevin O'Connell, who served as the Rams OC, where his offense went on to win the SuperBowl. O'Connell features a more pass-happy offense, which works perfectly with the second change in Justin Jefferson.

Jefferson has had no issues establishing himself as the Vikings WR1, breaking records as a rookie. Paired with O'Connell and Jefferson, Cousins is staffed with an elite supporting cast, allowing him to sling touchdowns and further up his PPR value.
