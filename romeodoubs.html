Romeo Doubs, Green Bay Packer, Wide Receiver

Doubs, drafted in 2022, in the 4th round by the Green Bay Packers, is a sleeper pick.

With Rodgers losing his perennial Pro-Bowler in Davante Adams, there remains a large void in the Packers WR core, comprised of Allen Lazard, Randall Cobb, and Christian Watson.

While training camp rumors are only rumors, footage from Doubs' training camp show an increased volume in targets, and more importantly him making tough, contested catches. Furthermore, there are reports of increased chemistry between Rodgers and Doubs, regularly grabbing meals together.
